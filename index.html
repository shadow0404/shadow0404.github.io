About Me

Anmol Rathi has worked on various projects with the most distinguished - Early Detection of Pancreatic Cancer through Lateral Flow Assay with application utilising retrospective data for risk assessment. He created a colorimetric strip-based test to detect Pancreatic Cancer using saliva. This strip is 125 times cheaper than MRI and CT scan and 35 times cheaper than CA19-9 Blood test. For his Innovation he has awarded Gold medal at Genius Olympiad 2019 , held at NewYork, USA. Also selected as regional finalist of Google Science Fair 2018. This project bagged 1st Prize of Rs. 1 Lakh in CSIR Innovation awards for school children 2019 at Vigyan Bhavan under the Ministry of Science and Technology. Bagged a Gold Medal at INSEF, organized by Science Society of India, First position at vision-2030 Challenge conducted by Techfest 2018, IIT Bombay.This project was also winner of techexpo 2019 held in IIT Guwahati. Also qualified to be presented at the Ethics Committee Meetings of - Rajiv Gandhi Cancer Institute & Research Centre, Delhi & AIIMS, Delhi.

His other eminent project was SPITCLEAVENT-A Scientific Approach to Clean and Prevent Salivary Ejection, a spit cleaning and preventing device. This project bagged 4th Prize of INR 20000/- in CSIR Innovation awards 2018 at Vigyan Bhavan. Also won Gold Medal in Science & Innovation Fair 2018, FGSI,Delhi. Got 4th Position at Techkriti 2018, IIT Kanpur. Also National Finalist of CBSE National Science Exhibition Feb-2018, Delhi.
